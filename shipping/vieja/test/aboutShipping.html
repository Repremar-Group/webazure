About Us

Originally conceived in 1996 as a division of REPREMAR Shipping to develop air cargo, ocean consolidated, and logistic services, REPREMAR Logistics became independent on May 1, 1999. Since then, REPREMAR Logistics has experienced an extraordinary development based on its dynamic management, its qualified and professional staff with a strong sense of duty, and its top-level network of global agents and representatives.

The full service we are offering is specifically designed to meet your business needs.

We are part of one of the local leading groups which provide shipping services, port operations, logistics and international cargo transportation in its different modes: air, sea and land. In a global world which has become more and more demanding and complex, we are committed to provide outstanding quality services driven by highly reliable processes.

PORT/HUSBANDING AGENT

At all Uruguayan ports and off-shore maritime areas, we provide services for all type of ships: container ships, bulk-carriers, cruises, tankers, LNGâ€™s, barges and lighters. REPREMAR has a well-trained and highly experienced operational staff. Our services include arrival and dispatch for ships, berth requests, pilotage and tugs services, crew attendance, repairs and spare parts, supplies, transhipments coordination, medical attention, bunkering and lubricants.

BACK-OFFICE

To the liner shipping companies which provide regular line container services, we offer all type of back-office services, such as: handling and processing all shipping documents for import/export activities, control of equipment and container logistics, collection of freight charges and remittances, issuance of pro forma invoices for every port of call, accounting services, management and settlement of accounts, dealing with claims and processing statistical reports.
