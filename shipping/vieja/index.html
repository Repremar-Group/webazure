REPREMAR Shipping / THE COMPANY


REPREMAR WAS FOUNDED IN 1946 AND SINCE THEN IT HAS BEEN PROVIDING SHIP AGENCY AND PORT SERVICES TO THE LEADING SHIPPING COMPANIES AND VESSELS WHICH OPERATE ON THE TIP OF THE EAST COAST OF SOUTH AMERICA AND THE RIVER PLATE.
Formerly, it belonged to the English Vestey Group, but in 1982 it was taken by the Pena family. Under the new management, the company was restructured and diversified its range of services and business units.
Currently, REPREMAR Shipping is part of one of the local leading groups which provide shipping services, port operations, logistics and international cargo transportation in its different modes: air, sea and land. In a global world which has become more and more demanding and complex, we are committed to provide outstanding quality services driven by highly reliable processes.
REPREMAR was the first shipping agency in Uruguay certified in Quality as it was awarded the ISO 9001-94 Quality Certificate in July 1999.
Our Quality and Management system is nowadays certified by the ISO 9001:2015 Certificate as well as TRACE Certified.

Exclusive Member in Uruguay of MULTIPORT

 

As from the 1st January, 2000, REPREMAR Shipping was appointed as exclusive member in Uruguay of MULTIPORT, the leading global network for shipping agencies. This network, which is the largest of its type, restored the trust and confidence in the ship agency profession by appointing exclusive members which represent its values: Quality, Integrity and Professionalism.


Services

PORT/HUSBANDING AGENT

At all Uruguayan ports and off-shore maritime areas, we provide services for all type of ships: container ships, bulk-carriers, cruises, tankers, LNG’s, barges and lighters.
REPREMAR has a well-trained and highly experienced operational staff.
Our services include arrival and dispatch for ships, berth requests, pilotage and tugs services, crew attendance, repairs and spare parts, supplies, transhipments coordination, medical attention, bunkering and lubricants.

BACK-OFFICE

To the liner shipping companies which provide regular line container services, we offer all type of back-office services, such as: handling and processing all shipping documents for import/export activities, control of equipment and container logistics, collection of freight charges and remittances, issuance of pro forma invoices for every port of call, accounting services, management and settlement of accounts, dealing with claims and processing statistical reports.

PORT OPERATIONS

Stevedoring, Loading/Discharging vessels,
logistics within Port premises,

Quality Policy

It is important to remark that REPREMAR Shipping was the first Uruguayan shipping agency certified in quality as it was awarded the ISO 9001 Certificate on August 2, 1999 for its Quality System.

We were pioneers within the local shipping community as we were the first agency to apply the Quality Management System to all our activities and procedures. Our Quality was certified by LATU (Uruguayan Technological Laboratory) in association with IQNET(International Certificate Network) and the Austrian Association for Certification of Quality.

In April 2009 we updated our SGC Certificate according to ISO 9001:2008. To further enhance our commitment with quality and transparency, we are also TRACE Certified.
